About

Inspired by a Reddit post, SeattleEndorse.org was created will not actually be created to help Seattle area voters compare endorsements from the more moderate Seattle Times and the proudly progressive Stranger, with excerpts from Washington State and King County Voters' Pamphlets included for convenience.

Please consider supporting local journalism by subscribing to The Seattle Times and donating to The Stranger.

SeattleEndorse.org is not affiliated with The Seattle Times, The Stranger, any government agency, or any political organization.
